What's FlowMotion™?

We're FlowMotion and as adventurers, travelers and techies, we believe that great moments should be captured in the best possible way. That’s why we made FlowMotion™ ONE - the world’s most versatile smartphone stabilizer.

FlowMotion™ ONE eliminates camera shake so you can capture every moment in perfect quality.

FlowMotion™ ONE only weighs 300g and fits in your pocket. Simply detach the grip and fold the stabilizer. So, basiclly, you can take it EVERYWHERE!
